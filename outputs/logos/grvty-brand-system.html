Mark Construction
Base Shape
Equilateral-ish triangle, apex pointing down
Points: (50,92) · (3,12) · (97,12) on 100×100 grid. The downward apex = gravity pulling things toward your business.
Inner Hollow
Smaller concentric triangle cutout
Points: (50,73) · (17,24) · (83,24). Creates depth — the mark has weight without being a solid blob.
Core Circle
Center of mass — the focal point
Center: (50,64) · Radius: 9 units. Represents the gravitational center — the invisible force.
Proportions
Mark : Wordmark = 1 : 4.5
In horizontal lockup, cap-height of wordmark = 80% of mark height. Never scale below 16px mark height.
Typeface
Bebas Neue · 0.08em letter-spacing
All caps. Never use any other typeface for the wordmark. Sub-labels use Inter 600 in all-caps.
